What is the main role of an Authorized Person?

The main role of an Authorized Person is to recruit clients under him & regulate their business, based on which brokerage will be generated.

Who is an Authorized Person?

An Authorized Person (formerly known as a Sub Broker) is a person who is registered with SEBI as such and is affiliated to a member of a recognised stock exchange.
